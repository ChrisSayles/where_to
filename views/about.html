An Overview of the Project

Application Concept

We use data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics to give users some insight into which cities might be the best fit for them. The first functionality of our application compares the average salary of the user's occupation in each city to the cost of living in that city. We display this "bang for your buck" ratio with a heatmap.


The second functionality of our application lets the user compare the cost of living categories between two cities. These cost of living indexes are displayed in an interactive donut chart"



Motivation Behind It

D3 is a popular data visualization library that is used by comapnies like The New York Times. Plus it looked like a lot of fun. We wanted to challenge ourselves to learn this powerful framework well enough to make something useful.
